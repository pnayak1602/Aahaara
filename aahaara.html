About us:
We are a Bangaluru based group of young minds working towards eradicating hunger . Our shared goal is to not let anyone go to sleep on an empty stomach. We connect people and food through donations,food banks and volunteering. We take nutritious food—before it goes to waste—from retailers, manufacturers, and farmers and gives it to people in need.
Team Aahaara:
On an average about 50kgs of food is wasted per person annually in India , but unfortunately more than 14% percent of our population (189.2 million people) is still fighting hunger every single day . Team Aahaara was established with the initiatve to address the issue of food wastage and acts as a connecting link between food donors and the needy. We are currently working on addressing immediate hunger needs by bringing together local stakeholders to work on ending hunger in their localities.
